Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

At this foundational stage, we nurture curiosity and creativity in young minds. Our curriculum focuses on building confidence, developing social skills, and fostering a love for learning through play-based and hands-on activities. We prepare children to explore their world with courage and compassion, setting a strong foundation for lifelong growth.

Elementary School

Our Elementary School program is designed to ignite imagination and cultivate critical thinking. Students engage in a balanced curriculum that emphasizes academic excellence, creativity, and personal growth. Through collaborative projects and interactive learning, we empower them to develop confidence and prepare for future challenges with curiosity and enthusiasm.

Middle (10-16) Years

The Middle School curriculum challenges students to explore their unique potential while building resilience and leadership skills. With a focus on academic rigor, innovation, and ethical values, we guide students to think critically, communicate effectively, and contribute meaningfully to a dynamic and diverse world.
